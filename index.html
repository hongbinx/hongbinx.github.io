Hongbin Xu 许鸿彬

Hongbin Xu is a keyboardist and producer based in Los Angeles. He earned a bachelor of music in keyboard performance in contemporary styles with a minor in audio production from the Musicians Institute. Since then, he has supported artists, including Zach Louis and Maya Kuriel. Outside of his musicianship on stage, his recording and production credits include Ann One, Killagramz, among others.
